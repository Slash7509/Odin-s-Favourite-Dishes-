Thor's Scrumptious Snack!

Ingredients:

  • 1 large turkey leg
    •
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional, for a spicy kick)
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
    •

Instructions:

  1. Prep the Turkey Leg:

    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • In a small bowl, mix together the smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, salt, and cayenne pepper (if using).

  2. Season the Turkey Leg:

    • Rub the turkey leg with olive oil, then coat it evenly with the spice mix.

  3. Bake:

    • Place the seasoned turkey leg on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or in a baking dish.
    • Bake in the preheated oven for about 45 minutes to 1 hour, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C) and the skin is crispy.

  4. Glaze:

    • In a small bowl, mix together the honey and mustard.
    • Brush the glaze over the turkey leg during the last 10 minutes of baking.

  5. Serve:

    • Remove the turkey leg from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.
    • Enjoy it hot, just as Thor would after a victory!

Optional Side:

  • For a quick side, serve with a handful of roasted nuts or a small bowl of fresh berries to balance the hearty flavor of the turkey leg.

This thunderous snack is perfect for a quick, satisfying bite that’s still worthy of a hero’s appetite.
